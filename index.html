About VFW Post 5436

Located in Troy, Ohio, VFW Post 5436 proudly serves veterans and the local community by providing support, fellowship, advocacy, and meaningful programs that honor those who served our country.

From hosting family-friendly events and community dinners to supporting scholarships and veteran initiatives, our mission is to keep service and patriotism alive in our community.

What We Do

Veteran Support

Helping local veterans access resources, support, and community.

Community Events

Hosting bingo nights, dinners, memorial events, and fundraisers.

Scholarship Programs

Supporting local youth through programs like Voice of Democracy.

Membership & Brotherhood

Building strong connections among veterans and families.

Voice of Democracy Scholarship

The nation's premier audio-essay scholarship competition. This year's theme: "What Has a Veteran Taught Me About America?"

Students in grades 9–12 record a 3–5 minute audio essay for a chance to compete for more than $2 million in scholarships and awards. Honor their legacy. Share your perspective.
